Some Hits

When the Lord

The song addresses introspective and personal themes, exploring the relationship between religion, spirituality and everyday life. The song questions the existence of a higher being and reflects on the complexities of faith and the search for meaning. Susanne Sundfør's distinctive voice and her musical style characterized by electronic and experimental elements combine to create a unique atmosphere on "When the Lord". The song showcases her ability to address deep issues in poetic and emotionally nuanced ways

White Foxes

Susanne Sundfør's song "Undercover" is part of her 2015 album titled "Ten Love Songs". The song deals with themes of desire, love, and hiding parts of oneself, both physically and emotionally. La canción juega con la idea de revelar los verdaderos sentimientos y deseos de manera gradual, como si estuviera revelando un secreto o estuviera involucrada en una especie de juego de rol. En general, "Undercover" es una canción que explora la complejidad de las relaciones humanas, los deseos ocultos y la necesidad de abrirse y ser auténtico con los demás.
